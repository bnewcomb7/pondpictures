Caleb McPhee

Producer, Pond Pictures LLC

Caleb McPhee (age 31) is an Eagle Scout from Troop 64 Saylesville, RI. He worked on Yawgoog Staff from 2011 to 2025, from Sandy Beach Services to Assistant Camp Director of Three Point.

Caleb graduated from Rhode Island College in 2018 with a BA in Theater Performance. He’s been making videos for almost ten years (six with Narragansett Council), accumulating over 3 million views on YouTube, and works professionally in Blu-ray production as an associate producer — making trailers, special features, and an interview series about the history of the Anime Business. He currently resides in East Providence.

A board member of the Yawgoog Alumni Association, Caleb is part of the Museum committee and has been cataloguing and archiving Yawgoog history. Last year he restored a 2.5-hour tour of Yawgoog featuring H. Cushman Anthony filmed in 1987, and digitized over 20 hours of VHS recordings from 1986–2003 of Yawgoog reunions, ceremonies, and more.
